10 reasons why the BackX Pack is the best city backpack for work and commuting


Premium BackX Pack
smart backpack with a USB port and a headphone plugin

A smart backpack is the perfect companion in today's urban environment for schoolchildren and students, office workers and IT professionals, as well as bankers or freelancers. In short, it is suitable for everyone who cares about the everyday safety of their belongings.

Stylish, practical, waterproof and watertight, the BackX Pack is a premium backpack with a code lock. Made of heavy-duty, wear-resistant fiber. The backpack is equipped with lots of compartments and pockets so you can fit absolutely everything you need. Don't worry about your belongings if you suddenly get caught in the rain. It's water resistant and the zippers are rubberized so no moisture can get in.

Here are 10 reasons why we think the BackX Pack is the only backpack you'll want to wear from 9 to 5 o'clock and beyond.

1. elegant and chic design for everyday wear.

The BackX Pack Pack has a thoughtful and elegant design that will fit both women's and men's styles.

2. More spacious than you think.

The design of internal compartments allows you to easily and securely place in the backpack: laptops, tablets, cameras, school supplies, clothes, a water bottle, acharger, headphones and all other small things you need during the day.

3. Unmatched quality.

The BackX Pack utilizes Anti-break composite technology that is unique in the textile industry. The cut-resistant material is a combination of ultra-high-molecular-weight polyethylene, fiberglass and a highly durable polyester. All of these fabrics have been combined to create an advanced, cut-resistant material.

4. Protect yourself from theft.

The BackX Pack features a snap fastener for zippers with a code lock, allowing the wearer to be confident of keeping their belongings safe wherever they are, whether it's during rush hour in transportation or elsewhere.

5. Stay connected to everyone wherever you are

The backpack has a dedicated USB port on the outside where you can plug in a cable and charge your smartphone, camera, tablet or laptop. The backpack also has an headphone plugin.

6. Ergonomics.

Backpack has a rigid frame, dense ergonomic backrest with soft breathable inserts. Wide shoulder straps are adjustable in length, evenly distribute the weight and make carrying a backpack comfortable even when fully loaded.

7. Waterproof

BackX Pack is made from waterproof fabric and the contents won't get wet even in heavy rain.

8. Weight distribution

Correct and straight posture is not only about appearance, but also about overall physical health. It reduces stress on the joints, muscles and spine and protects them from injury. When the weight in the backpack is incorrectly distributed, it can cause a twist in the spine. With the BackX Pack, this is not a problem.

9. Everywhere and everywhere

BackX Pack is your faithful companion both for travel and everyday use. The backpack is available in different colors: black, gray, blue, red, purple.

10. Organize your life

The BackX Pack has a thoughtfully organized compartment with multiple pockets for all your work items. Store your stuff in a well-organized way and you'll never have to dig through your bag to find a pen.

Another bonus is.
"Hidden Pocket."

We apply innovative ideas. In the strap there is a hidden pocket for quick access to various small things, such as: credit cards, money, keys.
